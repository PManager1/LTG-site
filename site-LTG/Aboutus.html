Mission Statement

The Home healthcare industry is undergoing a consolidation in which larger firms are looking to capture market share through the acquisition of smaller local and regional companies. We see an excellent opportunity for quantum growth in the chaos surrounding these mergers. Our goal is to acquire several companies, consolidate them to eliminate redundant operations, & grow as fast as humanly possible, and then take the organization public with an IPO.

Vision Statement

Liberty Trust Group's vision is to dominate and become a major force in determining the future of the HealthCare Industry in the USA. The time is right to aggressively acquire HealthCare companies with an intention of building a company comprising of 50-100 acquisitions and then taking it public.
